1. Introduction

What is debugging?

Not just finding bugs. Specifically, finding them by using tools that let you watch your code as it runs and manually control it line-by-line. These tools are called debuggers.

Advantages:

  • See the state (value) of your variables at particular times during your program.
  • Pause your program at a particular place or on a particular error.
  • Run an expression or statement in the paused program to try things out.
  • (Web/javascript) Inspect and manipulate HTML and CSS elements.
